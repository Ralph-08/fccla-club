About FCCLA

FCCLA stands for Family, Career and Community of America. FCCLA is an organization that has been around since 1945. This is a national Career and Technical Student Organization for students in public or private schools through 12th grade.

This organization has been making a difference in families, careers and communities for years. And continues until this day to keep making a difference in families and communities. FCCLA has over 237,000 student members and more than 7,000 chapter advisers across 5,100 chapters.
